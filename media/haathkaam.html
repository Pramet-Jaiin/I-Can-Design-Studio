What we did

We partnered with Haathkaam, a fashion studio rooted in vibrant Indian craftsmanship, to elevate their brand presence. Our work began with designing a distinctive logo—a minimalist yet impactful design featuring a black bird carrying a pink flower. The logo symbolizes elegance, creativity, and the delicate artistry Haathkaam stands for.


For their social media, we developed an array of visually rich creatives. Each post highlighted their handcrafted apparel with attention to detail, vivid colors, and thoughtful captions that reflect their dedication to transforming traditional fabrics into modern masterpieces. This cohesive visual strategy strengthened their connection with their audience.
